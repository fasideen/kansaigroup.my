    Who We Are

    DRIVEN BY TRUST,
    BUILT IN JAPAN

    Kansai Group Corporation was founded in Osaka, Japan — the heartland of Japan's automotive trade. For over 35 years, we have been exporting premium Japanese vehicles to markets across Asia, earning a reputation for transparency, quality, and unmatched selection.

    Our new representative office at 3 Towers, Jalan Ampang, Kuala Lumpur marks our commitment to the Malaysian market. We bring Japan's most trusted auto auction experience directly to Malaysian buyers — both businesses and individuals.

    Whether you are a dealer looking to source quality stock, or a buyer seeking your dream car at the right price, Kansai Group Corporation is your trusted Japanese automotive partner in Malaysia.
